Target Audience
This site's target audience is for people who not only want to know the weather in their area, but also what the weather is for any friends/family they have in two other cities! They can also use this website when they visit their friends/family in the two other cities. If I could, I would make it so they can select whatever cities they want, but for the sake of the assignment it's targeted to people who live in either Ridgecrest, Salem, or Kennewick, and then have friends/family in two of these cities.

Two personas
